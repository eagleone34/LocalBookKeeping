Common Questions

Is LocalBooks a good alternative to QuickBooks for small businesses?
Yes. LocalBooks offers the core features small businesses need — income and expense tracking, bank statement import, financial reports, and budget management — for a one-time payment of $49.99 instead of $30+/month. Your data stays on your computer, not in the cloud.
Do I need an internet connection to use LocalBooks?
No. LocalBooks runs entirely on your desktop. All your data is stored locally in a secure database on your machine. No internet connection is required to use any feature.
What bank statements can LocalBooks import?
LocalBooks can import PDF bank statements from major Canadian and US banks including RBC, TD, BMO, Scotiabank, CIBC, Chase, and more. Simply drag and drop your statement PDF, and transactions are extracted and auto-categorized.
Is my financial data secure with LocalBooks?
Absolutely. Your data never leaves your computer. There is no cloud sync, no tracking, and no third-party access. LocalBooks uses a secure SQLite database stored locally on your machine with WAL (Write-Ahead Logging) for data integrity.
What happens if I need help or have a problem?
Reach out to us at support@llmboost.ca. We respond to all support requests within 24 hours on business days.
Will LocalBooks work on Mac?
LocalBooks currently supports Windows 10 and Windows 11. Mac support is on our roadmap. If you purchase now, the Mac version will be included free when it's available.
Can I import data from QuickBooks or other software?
You can import your bank statements as PDFs to quickly populate your transaction history. Direct QuickBooks data import is on our roadmap for a future update.
What is included in the $49.99 price?
Everything. All current features plus all future updates are included. There are no tiers, no add-ons, and no recurring fees. One payment gives you lifetime access to LocalBooks, including multi-company support, unlimited transactions, and every future version.
